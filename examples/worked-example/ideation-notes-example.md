# Ideation Notes Example

**Version:** 1.1  
**Date:** 2026-03-30  
**Status:** Worked example  
**Method Version:** 1.1

## Idea Summary

- Concept: Track books to read, in progress, and finished
- Audience: One person using it for personal organization
- Why now: Existing notes are scattered and inconsistent

## Goals

- Primary goal: Replace ad hoc notes with a simple structured tracker
- Secondary goals: Learn the workflow and keep requirements small

## Risks and Unknowns

- Risk 1: Overbuilding features before the first useful version exists
- Risk 2: Turning a personal tracker into a social recommendation app too early
- Open question 1: Should notes be freeform only or partially structured?

## Early Scope

- In scope: book list, reading status, start and finish dates, notes
- Out of scope: sharing, recommendations, syncing across users

## Next Questions to Resolve

- How much metadata is actually needed for the first milestone?
- What is the simplest workflow for adding and updating a book?

## Decision Snapshot

- What feels solid: personal tool, minimal first milestone, documentation-first workflow
- What needs more pressure testing: exact data model and first implementation slice
